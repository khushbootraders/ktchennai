About Khushboo Traders

Khushboo Traders is a trusted wholesale and retail supplier of premium quality spices, dry fruits, nuts, seeds, makhana and asafoetida (hing) located in the heart of Sowcarpet, Chennai.

We are committed to delivering quality products, competitive pricing and reliable service to retailers, wholesalers, businesses and individual customers.
